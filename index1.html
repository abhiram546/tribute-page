About Junior NTR

Junior NTR, also known as Tarak, is an Indian film actor, dancer, and playback singer known for his work in Telugu cinema. He made his acting debut as a child artist in the film Brahmarshi Viswamitra in 1991 and later appeared in films such as Ramayanam, Student No. 1, and Aadi. He received critical acclaim for his performances in films like Simhadri, Yamadonga, and Temper. Junior NTR is a recipient of several awards, including four Nandi Awards and two Filmfare Awards.

Aside from his acting career, Junior NTR is also a trained Kuchipudi dancer and has performed in several stage shows. He has also lent his voice for various songs in Telugu films.
